Our Specialties

  • 🥜 Plain Chana Simple roasted or boiled chickpeas without any seasoning, great as a healthy snack or ingredient in various dishes.
  • 🌶️Masala Chana Chickpeas seasoned with a mix of Indian spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder, giving them a flavorful and aromatic taste.
  • 🔥 Peri-Peri Chana Spicy and tangy chickpeas infused with peri-peri seasoning, which typically includes paprika, garlic, chili flakes, and lemon zest for a fiery kick.
  • 🍛Chole ChanaA popular North Indian dish where chickpeas are cooked in a rich, spicy, and tangy tomato-based gravy with traditional Indian spices.
  • 🧄Garlic Chana Chickpeas flavored with roasted or fried garlic, giving them a bold and savory taste, often enjoyed as a crunchy snack or stir-fried side dish.
  • 🍋Lemon Chana Chickpeas seasoned with lemon juice, salt, and mild spices, offering a tangy and refreshing flavor, perfect for a light and zesty snack.
  • 🎁 Chikki is a traditional Indian sweet made from jaggery (gur) and nuts or seeds, most commonly peanuts (moongfali chikki). It's a crunchy and nutritious snack enjoyed during winter, especially during Makar Sankranti.
  • Peanut Chikki – Made with roasted peanuts and jaggery, the most popular variety.
  • Til (Sesame) Chikki – Made with sesame seeds and jaggery, offering a nutty flavor.
  • Dry Fruit Chikki – A mix of almonds, cashews, pistachios, and sometimes figs or dates.
  • Coconut Chikki – Made with grated coconut and jaggery for a rich and sweet taste.
  • Mix Seeds Chikki – Contains flaxseeds, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, and jaggery for a healthy snack.
  • Chocolate Chikki – A fusion version with cocoa or chocolate syrup added to the traditional chikki.
  • 🍏 Kacchi Kairi (Raw Mango) A tangy and slightly sour flavor, often used in summer drinks, candies, and chaat masala for a refreshing kick.
  • 🍓 StrawberryA juicy, slightly tart, and sweet flavor, popular in candies, milkshakes, and jams.
  • 🌰Imli (Tamarind) A sour-sweet and tangy flavor, widely used in chutneys, golgappa water, and candies for a bold taste.
  • 🍐 Guava A mild, sweet, and slightly tangy flavor, commonly used in juices, candies, and jams.
